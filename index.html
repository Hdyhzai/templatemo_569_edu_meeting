WHY WE SHOULD DO EARLY PREDICTION?

Early Detection

Predicting heart disease early allows for timely medical intervention,

reducing the risk of severe complications.

Preventive Action

Identifying risks early enables personalized lifestyle adjustments and treatments, improving overall health outcomes.

Reduced Disease Progression

Timely interventions slow or halt disease progression, lowering hospitalization rates and preventing advanced conditions.

Cost Savings

Early prediction minimizes expensive treatments and hospital stays, reducing the financial burden on patients and healthcare systems.

Efficient Resource Allocation

Optimized use of medical resources improves healthcare services and supports a broader population, enhancing public health initiatives.
